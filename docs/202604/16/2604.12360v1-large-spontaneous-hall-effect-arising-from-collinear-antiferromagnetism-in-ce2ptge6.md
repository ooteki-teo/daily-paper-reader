## 摘要
自发霍尔效应，即零场反常霍尔效应（AHE），通常由铁磁性相关的对称性破缺所诱发。然而，近年来的研究表明，那些具有允许铁磁性的磁点群特征的反铁磁（AFM）态，同样可以破坏相关对称性并在没有大净磁化的情况下诱导出AHE。本文报道了反铁磁体系Ce$_2$PtGe$_6$表现出显著的自发霍尔效应。单晶中子散射实验证实，Ce$_2$PtGe$_6$具有传播矢量$q=0$的共线反铁磁结构。其约$\sim 10^{-3}$ $μ_B$/Ce的微小净磁化强度表明，观测到的AHE源于其反铁磁结构固有的对称性破缺。反常霍尔电导率（AHC）高达$300$ $Ω^{-1}$cm$^{-1}$，超过了相关化合物如Ce$_2$CuGe$_6$和Ce$_2$PdGe$_6$的本征AHC。这一巨大的AHC很可能归因于Pt原子强大的自旋轨道耦合，为理解贝里曲率与具有反铁磁构型的局域化f电子矩之间的相互作用提供了一个平台。

## Abstract
The spontaneous Hall effect, corresponding to a zero-field anomalous Hall effect (AHE), is induced by symmetry breaking associated with ferromagnetism. Studies in recent years, however, have revealed that antiferromagnetic (AFM) states characterized by magnetic point groups that allow ferromagnetism can also break the relevant symmetries and induce AHE without a large net magnetization. Here, we report that the AFM system Ce$_2$PtGe$_6$ exhibits a pronounced spontaneous Hall effect. Single-crystal neutron scattering experiments demonstrate that Ce$_2$PtGe$_6$ exhibits a collinear AFM structure with a propagation vector $q=0$. The small net magnetization of $\sim 10^{-3}$ $μ_B$/Ce indicates that the observed AHE arises from symmetry breaking inherent to its AFM structure. The anomalous Hall conductivity (AHC) reaches $300$ $Ω^{-1}$cm$^{-1}$, which exceeds the intrinsic AHC of related compounds such as Ce$_2$CuGe$_6$ and Ce$_2$PdGe$_6$. This large AHC, most likely attributed to the large spin-orbit coupling of the Pt atoms, provides a platform for understanding the interplay between the Berry curvatures and localized $f$-moments with an AFM configuration.
