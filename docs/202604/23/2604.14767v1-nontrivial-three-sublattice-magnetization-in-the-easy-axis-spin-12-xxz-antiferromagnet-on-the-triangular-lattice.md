## 摘要
我们利用密度矩阵重整化群研究了三角晶格上自旋-1/2 XXZ反铁磁体在易轴区域的基态磁结构。通过施加螺旋边界条件，我们将有限的L×L团簇精确映射到一维链上，同时避免了圆柱几何中固有的空间各向异性。从对称性破缺的局域磁化分布中，我们提取了三子晶格磁矩并追踪其随各向异性的演化。在各向同性点，我们得到了正子晶格磁矩为0.21671，与先前的数值估计一致。在易轴区域，有序磁矩在较宽的Δ范围内保持接近零总磁矩的三子晶格结构形式(2m,-m,-m)。通过1/Δ外推显示，正子晶格磁矩远低于经典饱和值1/2，当Δ→∞时趋近于0.41873，而负子晶格磁矩的大小趋近于0.20832。我们进一步比较了Y态和上下下态的能量，发现零场下Y态更有利。在不假定任何特定有序模式的情况下进行的独立热力学极限能量计算，得到的能量与Y态解一致。这些结果表明，易轴基态并非简单地过渡到平庸饱和的共线伊辛态，而是保持为由量子涨落从宏观简并的伊辛流形中选择出的非平庸三子晶格有序态。

## Abstract
We investigate the ground-state magnetic structure of the spin-$1/2$ XXZ antiferromagnet on the triangular lattice in the easy-axis regime using the density-matrix renormalization group. By applying spiral boundary conditions, we exactly map finite $L\times L$ clusters onto one-dimensional chains while avoiding the spatial anisotropy inherent in cylindrical geometries. From symmetry-broken local magnetization profiles, we extract the three-sublattice moments and track their evolution with anisotropy. At the isotropic point, we obtain a positive sublattice moment of $0.21671$, consistent with previous numerical estimates. In the easy-axis regime, the ordered moments remain close to a zero-magnetization three-sublattice structure of the form $(2m,-m,-m)$ over a broad range of $Δ$. Extrapolation in $1/Δ$ shows that the positive sublattice moment stays well below the classical saturation value $1/2$, approaching $0.41873$ as $Δ\to\infty$, while the magnitude of the negative sublattice moment approaches $0.20832$. We further compare the energies of the Y state and the up-down-down state and find that the Y state is favored at zero field. Independent thermodynamic-limit energy calculations, performed without assuming any particular ordered pattern, yield an energy consistent with the Y-state solution. These results show that the easy-axis ground state does not simply cross over to a trivially saturated collinear Ising state, but instead remains a nontrivial three-sublattice ordered state selected from the macroscopically degenerate Ising manifold by quantum fluctuations.
