## 摘要
我们研究了三角晶格上的一个有效低能近藤模型，其中巡游电子占据布里渊区Γ点的一个价带口袋和M点的三个导带口袋。该结构具有费米面嵌套特征，在仅考虑低能带结构的情况下倾向于形成三重Q磁有序。将局域磁矩处理为四子格磁单胞中的经典自旋，我们发现了扩展的非共面有序区域，包括四面体态及相关倾斜四面体态，此外还存在铁磁和共面相。手性相在广泛的袋间近藤耦合范围内保持稳定，并在外磁场存在下持续存在。对于某些手性有序，电子能带可打开能隙并呈现量子反常霍尔态，其霍尔电导为σ_{xy}=4e^2/h。这些结果表明，三角晶格上的手性磁性和量子化反常霍尔效应并不依赖于特定的紧束缚能带结构，而可以更普遍地源于Γ点和M点的低能嵌套口袋。

## Abstract
We study an effective low-energy Kondo model on the triangular lattice in which itinerant electrons occupy a valence pocket at $Γ$ and three conduction pockets at the $M$ points of the Brillouin zone. This construction has a Fermi-surface nesting structure that favors triple-$Q$ magnetic order while only assuming the low-energy band-structure. Treating the local moments as classical spins on a four-sublattice magnetic unit cell, we find extended regions of non-coplanar order, including tetrahedral and related canted tetrahedral states, in addition to ferromagnetic and coplanar phases. The chiral phases remain stable over a broad range of inter-pocket Kondo couplings and persist in the presence of an external magnetic field. For certain chiral orders, the electronic bands can become gapped and host a quantum anomalous Hall state with $σ_{xy}=4\,e^2/h$. These results show that chiral magnetism and a quantized anomalous Hall effect on the triangular lattice do not rely on a specific tight-binding band structure, but can arise more generally from low-energy nested pockets at $Γ$ and $M$.
